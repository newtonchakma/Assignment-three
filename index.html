Frequently Asked Questions

There is nothing worse than attending a convention only to be crammed into small meeting rooms with other attendees. Make sure the rooms will be large enough to accommodate the number of visitors scheduled to attend.

These days, guests are looking for more than just a place to sleep at night. Make sure the hotel you’re considering is competitive when it comes to freebies and amenities. High-speed wireless Internet, a business center, spacious rooms, and an onsite health club are just a few of the things your convention attendees will be looking for.

Make sure the sites you’re considering offer a range of services, including meals and refreshments, and that there will be waiters and other support staff on hand to provide these services. Read How to Get a Convention off the Ground for more help on this topic.
